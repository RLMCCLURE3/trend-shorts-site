Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 2, 2026

1. Overview

Trend Shorts Publisher ("the Tool") is a personal automation application operated by a single individual (the "Operator") to publish content to the Operator's own social media accounts. It has no public users, no accounts, and no sign-up. This policy explains what limited data the Tool handles.

2. Data the Tool accesses

Through platform APIs (such as the TikTok Content Posting API), the Tool accesses only the Operator's own account, limited to: basic profile information of the Operator's account, and the ability to upload and publish video content created by the Operator. The Tool does not access, collect, or store data about any other person or any other account.

3. Data storage

OAuth access tokens for the Operator's accounts are stored locally on the Operator's own computer, within the automation software's encrypted credential store. Generated content and publishing logs are stored locally on the same machine. No data is transmitted to any party other than the social media platforms being published to and the AI services used to generate content.

4. Data sharing and sale

The Tool does not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties. No advertising or analytics services are used on this site or in the Tool.

5. Data deletion

All data handled by the Tool can be deleted by the Operator at any time by revoking the app's access in the connected platform's settings and deleting local files. To request deletion of anything related to this Tool, contact the address below.

6. Cookies

This website is a static informational page and sets no cookies.
